About Tiation macOS Toolkit

The Tiation macOS Toolkit is a comprehensive collection of intelligent zsh completion files designed specifically for macOS developers and system administrators. Our toolkit dramatically enhances terminal productivity by providing context-aware completions for native macOS commands and popular third-party tools.

Built with enterprise requirements in mind, our toolkit provides the reliability, performance, and comprehensive coverage that professional development teams need to streamline their workflows.
